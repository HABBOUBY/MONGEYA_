
MONGEYA - POWER DISTRIBUTION

Battery: 3S LiPo (11.1V)

Main Power Flow:
3S LiPo
  -> Power Switch
  -> Fuse (optional safety)
  -> 12V Rail
      -> Vacuum Pump (12V)
      -> Chemical Pumps (12V via MOSFET)
      -> Motors Driver (JGB37)
  -> 5V Regulator (Buck Converter)
      -> ESP32-WROOM-32
      -> Sensors (IR, buttons)
      -> LiDAR (if 5V model)

Ground:
All systems share COMMON GND
